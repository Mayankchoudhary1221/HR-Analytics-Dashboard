# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive data visualization project developed using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze workforce data and identify key factors affecting employee attrition.

This dashboard helps HR teams and business leaders gain insights into employee demographics, salary trends, job satisfaction, and attrition patterns to support **data-driven HR decision-making**.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze employee demographics and workforce distribution
- Identify key factors influencing employee attrition
- Monitor salary trends and job satisfaction levels
- Provide actionable insights to improve employee retention

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Employee Overview
This section provides a snapshot of the organization's workforce.

Key Metrics:
- Total Employees
- Average Salary
- Average Age
- Monthly Salary Expense
- Average Salary Hike
- Job Satisfaction Score
- Gender Ratio

Visual Insights:
- Employee Count by Education Field
- Employee Distribution by Age Group
- Department-wise Workforce Distribution
- Salary vs Age Analysis
- Job Role Level Workforce Insights

---

### 2️⃣ Attrition Analysis
This section focuses on identifying patterns and factors contributing to employee attrition.

Key Metrics:
- Attrition Rate
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Job Role

Visual Insights:
- Attrition Rate by Age
- Attrition Rate by Gender
- Attrition Rate by Business Travel
- Attrition Rate by Education Field
- Attrition vs Job Satisfaction
- Attrition vs Monthly Income

---

## 🚀 Key Insights
- Higher attrition observed in younger employees
- Sales department shows comparatively higher attrition rates
- Employees with lower job satisfaction tend to leave more frequently
- Salary shows a positive relationship with employee age
